Rooftop and Spa

Experience unparalleled relaxation and entertainment at Xcess Rooftop and Spa, a luxurious retreat offering breathtaking city skyline views. Located atop a stylish low-rise building, our spa features comprehensive wellness amenities, including two hot tubs with unique ambiances, a tranquil relaxation pool, and two therapeutic saunas designed to detoxify and rejuvenate.

Enhance your visit with our full-service bar, serving a curated selection of cocktails, fine wines, and refreshing beverages. Whether you prefer to sip a drink in the sauna's lounge area, our bar adds a touch of sophistication to your spa experience. Additionally, our open-air bar provides the perfect setting to enjoy your favorite sport with a cocktail in hand.

Explore our second location, Excess Rooftop, at 318, 501 Thappraya Road in Pattaya. This venue offers stylish décor, comfortable seating, and stunning city views, making it an ideal spot to unwind. Known for its expertly mixed cocktails, Excess Rooftop combines classic favorites with innovative creations to enhance your sophisticated experience.

Discover a sanctuary of relaxation and indulgence at Xcess Rooftop and Spa, where wellness and social spaces blend seamlessly to offer the perfect urban escape. Plus, you can book your private party or event with us, ensuring a memorable and exclusive experience for you and your guests.
