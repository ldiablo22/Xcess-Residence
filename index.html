About Us

Welcome to Xcess Residence, Rooftop and Spa, a charming boutique hotel nestled in the serene Pratumnak Hill area of Pattaya. With just 15 elegantly designed rooms, we offer an intimate and personalized retreat for travelers seeking comfort and tranquility. Our prime location provides easy access to both the vibrant nightlife of Pattaya and the pristine beaches of Jomtien, ensuring you experience the best of both worlds. Whether you are here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo adventure, Xcess Residence, Rooftop and Spa is your perfect home away from home, where luxury meets convenience. Join us for an unforgettable stay, where every detail is designed to exceed your expectations.
